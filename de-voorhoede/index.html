Positioning the De Voorhoede as a creative partner

The creative blueprint, gets to the heart of what the De Voorhoede do. They transform plans into functioning interactive products. As front end specialists for the blueprint ‘blue’ there could only be one choice: html color-name blue.

product

Content you want to read

Improving the acessiblility of content was a top priority. For case studies I limited line lengths to a reading optimised 9-11 words per line. In addition I used larger type to reduce strain and generous line spacing to make each line of text easy for the reader to navigate. On mobile line spacing reduces significantly this is to counter the effect of shorter line-lengths that occur on small screens.

brand

‘See how its done’ mode

The majority of work that goes into a website will never be seen by the end user. For a FE development company however we wanted to showcase the elegant solutions that front enders create everyday. Enter the 'see how its done' feature. With one click user can reveal the underlying features that drive the DeVoorhoede.nl experience.
