Digital currencies. Simplified.

Digital currencies, simplified* — a vision to bring crypto currencies to the everyday person. To achieve this I created a brand identity that directly countered the preconceptions of crypto = complex. Working in a tight nit team of devs and UXers we created an intuitive, product experience that promised to simplify digital currencies.


*Note: The brand name has been changed and omitted due to standing NDA.

A simple propositon

Creating a consumer brand for a tech industry I wanted the brand to immediately set the users mind at ease. With this in mind I took direct inspiration from consumer brands and began with a proposition that was accessible from the word go.

brand

Just enough brand

The goal of the visual language was first and fore most to present information clearly. Brand elements are paired back injecting dashes of personality, but not so much that they ever got in the way.
