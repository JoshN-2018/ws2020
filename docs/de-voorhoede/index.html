Content you want to read

Improving the acessiblility of content was a top priority. For case studies I limited line lengths to a reading optimised 9-11 words per line. In addition I used larger type to reduce strain and generous line spacing to make each line of text easy for the reader to navigate. On mobile line spacing reduces significantly this is to counter the effect of shorter line-lengths that occur on small screens.
