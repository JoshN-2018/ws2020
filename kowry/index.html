Kowry

Kowry had a vision to bring crypto currencies to the every day person. To achieve this I built a brand that directly confronted users preconceptions of difficulty. From this base I developed a device independent visual language that negotiated the complex demands of marketing, financial data and dashboard design.

Brand Identity Product Design Motion Design

Just enough brand

To counter the crypto = complicated problem. I created a paired back brand identity and UI that was simplicity itself. The idea was to inject dashes of personality here and there, but not so much that it ever got in the way.

One system to rule them all

Kowry was built as a progressive web app. This presented a unique challenge in design. The look and feel of marketing, the product itself and the presentation financial data had to be tackled in one design system accomadating every device.

Content optimised

Using components content was optimized at every screen size.

Mobile first

We believed most users would access the service on mobile. Therefore the mantra 'if you can do it, you can do it on your phone' applied. All interactive elements were optimised to be at their best in this space critical environment.

A conversational user experience

Trading can be a complicated business. By utilising an if-this-then-that approach with our interface design and copy; trading became more conversation than chore.

Forms that dont waste your time

It was vital that initial impressions were of low effort. To ensure this I designed a form system that gave contextual feedback every step of the way.

Inline validation

Designed to scale

With so many potential user situations a tightly controlled design system was integral to the maintainability of the product. Working in close collaboration with development I created systems that ensured components adapted seemlessly and that those systems were efficient to implement.
