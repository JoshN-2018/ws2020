Concept storyline

Moto X Battle

Redbull athlete (Tarah Gieger) has challenged you to an all out race. Luckily between your phone and the can of Redbull, you already have everything you need to take her on. The experience places you directly in the riders seat with naturalistic controls as you take on Tarah on the circuits she's about to race in. Rider gives users their “Inner Circle” energy drink redemption codes as rewards for winning races and frequent play.

Core experience

Tilting the phone to the left or turns the bike. Rotating the can while in view of the camera accelerates the bike — exactly like a motorbike throttle.

Concept storyline

What the truck!?

Redbull Athlete (Ryan Sheckler) challenges you to learn his favorite moves. An AR overlay of the athlete’s feet on the board helps users every step of the way. When users share successful moves with #whatthetruck, they can win a sample pack of Red Bull cans and merch. Users purchase regionally specific cans to unlock advanced trick tutorials. Athletes teaching the tricks are specific to the user’s region.

Core experience

Computer vision tracks the shape of the users board and overlays correct foot placement throughout the stages of the trick. Users can scrub back and forth through the steps, or play all at once to get the timing. A voiceover bit the athlete guides you through each step.

Concept storyline

Flug 'that'

The RedBull event 'Flugtag' challenges people to build human-powered flying machines and launch them off an 8.5m flight deck. But what if none of those elements are available!? 'Flugtag 'that'' offers all the gratuitous pleasure without the admin. Simply choose your favourite flying machine and the first launch platform you see and AR will take care of the rest.

Core experience

After selecting their flying machine. Users point their phones at their desired launch platform and specify the launching point and ground by dragging markers to those positions. They then hit launch.

Concept storyline

Circuit breaker

'Circuit breaker' (for use at RedBull music events) is a promotion that enables users to make a great music moment absolutely spectacular. When the music is at its peak, fans press the Circuit breaker. When enough fans press it within the same time window, a circuit breaker is triggered: Then things are turned up to 11 — volume, lights, flares, all elements are used to elevate the moment further. Both the effects and the knowledge that it was collectively triggered build the atmosphere and sense of community are amplified. Red Bull and merch awarded for participation and for consistent taste.

Core experience

Microphone constantly tracks volume on app-screen (just for visual effect) when users think there experiencing a circuit breaker moment they can hit the CB button on the app. These presses are collected in real time and measured against a predetermined threshold.

Concept storyline

Drift pro

'Drift Pro' (for use at RedBull rally events) is a promotion that offers rally driver style drifting experience by substituting the users phone for the car itself. Energy drinks are earned by frequent users, and top scorers are given a paid trip to the rally of their choice — with a chance to meet the Red Bull drivers.

Core experience

After choosing a vehicle users place their device on flat surface and turn in order to control the drift and keep the car on track. Extra points are awarded for being closer to the ideal racing line.

Concept storyline

Step up to the plate

''Step up to the plate' (for use with any RedBull sport/athlete) is a promotion that offers the ability to directly compete against a favourite athlete. By using the accelerometer to count the users daily steps vs. Red Bull athletes. Beyond getting encouraged to move by notifications indicating how close they are to the steps of stars like Verstappen, steps become a pseudo-currency to be exchanged for spot prizes like cans of Red Bull.

Core experience

Choose a RedBull athlete, and begin competing. Users receive realtime notifications and be able to compare their progress vs athletes via an in app dashboard.
